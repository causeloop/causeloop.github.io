**Tips for an on-brand result**
- Use a **square** crop (≈ 400×400 or larger). The card crops it to a circle.
- A plain or softly-lit background works best; the avatar already sits in a
  navy→cyan brand frame with a terracotta accent dot.
- For the cleanest "brand background" blend, export a **background-removed PNG**
  (transparent) named `*.png` and update the `src` extension — the brand
  gradient will then show through behind the person.
