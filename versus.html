Causeloop is the AI-native intelligence layer that finds patterns across your issues, predicts recurrence, and proves sustainability. It is not a system of record, and it is not trying to be one — legacy GRC platforms are genuinely good at workflow, controls libraries, and being the authoritative store for individual issues.

Use Causeloop alongside your GRC system when you need cross-system patterns, recurrence prediction, and regulator-grade sustainability evidence. Consider it a replacement for the manual analysis — the spreadsheets, the consultant engagements, the quarterly fire drills — that exist today precisely because your current tools can’t connect the dots.

Causeloop doesn’t replace your GRC system. It makes it answer the questions your regulators are now asking.
